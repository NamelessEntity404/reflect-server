Case studies — problem / solution

What I built

A

Queer Fashion Brand

The problem

  • Queer-centered clothing design is nearly nonexistent
  • Menswear patterns don't fit queer bodies
  • Queer culture is absent from mainstream advertising
  • Existing queer brands lack inspirational copy — no "just do it" for us

The solution

  • A queer brand that helps you accomplish your goals, not just dress you
  • Clothes re-patterned to actually fit queer bodies
  • Copy that inspires beyond the garment
B

Abuse Detection
Smart Glasses

The problem

  • Smart glasses can save lives — they don't
  • The legal system is stacked against victims of abuse
  • Coercive control is hard to prove on long time horizons
  • Evidence can't be captured on demand in acute crisis situations

The solution

  • Glasses that activate and record when abuse is detected — zero attacker-facing cues
  • Documents save to tamper-proof cloud in a timeline designed for court
  • Coercive control becomes falsifiable with timestamps preserved for chain of custody
